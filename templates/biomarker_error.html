SmartQuery

Users can upload TXT files containing patient mutation information. These TXT files are compatible with the mutation format of the Cancer Genome Interpreter database. Subsequently, the database website can search biomarkers presenting in the mutation profile and display feasible drug combination regimens according to prioritization scores from high to low, enabling the selection of high-confidence drug combination therapies for patients. Additionally, users have the option to download the retrieved data.
